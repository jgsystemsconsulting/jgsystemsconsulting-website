### MBSE & SysML consulting

Model-based systems engineering done properly: SysML v1 and v2 in CATIA Magic / Cameo,
architecture frameworks, traceability, and model audits. We help teams stand up a repeatable,
model-driven practice and keep their models clean and reviewable.

### AI for engineering

Bring AI agents to your engineering work. We build MCP tooling and Claude Code integrations that
let agents query, audit, and edit live engineering models and apply your standards, over a local,
air-gapped connection. No cloud, no export.

### Tooling & products

Licensed and open tooling: MCP bridges for SysML, skills packs that distil vetted SE standards,
and apps such as VoiceFlow. Available across free, pro, and enterprise tiers. See Products for
the current catalogue.
