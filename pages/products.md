# Products

**Section:** § 01 - PRODUCTS
**Purpose:** Narrative intro to the product catalogue. The actual list is generated from

## Intro

Our public work is on GitHub, each product with its own live page. Everything is free to use
unless marked otherwise; pro and enterprise tiers add write-access and advanced tooling. Each card
links to the product page (Open) and its source repo (Source). The catalogue is kept current by
the refresh-website skill.
