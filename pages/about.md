## About

JG Systems Consulting Ltd is an independent systems architecture and MBSE consultancy. JGSC Labs
is where we open-source the tooling we build for our own client work: bridges that connect AI
agents to live SysML models in CATIA Magic, vetted systems-engineering knowledge packs, and
focused skills for Claude Code.

Everything here is built to run where engineering work actually happens, behind the firewall and
on real models, not demos. The tools are local-first and air-gapped by default: your models and
data never leave your machine. Most are free; a few are licensed. All of them come from a working
MBSE practice, not a lab.
