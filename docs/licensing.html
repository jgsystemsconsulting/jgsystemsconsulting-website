§ 02 - WHY A LICENCE

Why these are licensed

The free tools are read-only or generic: they navigate, inspect, audit, and report. The licensed tools are the ones that change real models — write-tier authoring and framework-specific validation (SysML, ArchiMate, NAF, UAF). That capability takes the most work to build correctly and carries the most risk if it gets it wrong, so it ships under a paid licence with support attached.

A licence keeps the project funded and the tools maintained against new releases of CATIA Magic, MSOSA, and Sparx EA. It also means you get a person to email when something breaks.
